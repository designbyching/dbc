The Art of Logo Design

Published: April 19, 2025

A great logo is more than just a pretty image—it’s a visual representation of a brand’s identity. At Design by Ching, we approach logo design with a focus on simplicity, versatility, and impact.

Our process involves understanding the client’s vision, researching their industry, and crafting a logo that communicates their values effectively. Whether it’s a minimalist icon or a bold typographic mark, we ensure every logo is timeless and adaptable.
